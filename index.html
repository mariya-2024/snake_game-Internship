How to Play

🎮 Controls

  • ↑ ↓ ← → or W A S D — Move snake
  • P — Pause / Resume

🐸 The Frog

  • Eat frogs to score +10 points
  • After a few static frogs, the next one can jump to escape!
  • Higher difficulty = frogs jump more often & more times

🐍 Guard Snakes (Hard only)

  • Two guards patrol near the frog vertically
  • Touching a guard = instant game over

🌙 Day / Night Cycle

  • World shifts: ☀️ Day → 🌆 Evening → 🌙 Night
  • At night, visibility shrinks around your snake
